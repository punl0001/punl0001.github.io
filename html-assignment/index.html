About Me

  • Favorite tags

The sun was our god, the mountains our father and the earth our mother. Tahuantinsuyo was the land of hunters, farmers, warriors and stargazers, this is how we did it in Peru before we were massacred and conquered by the spanish in a battle of arrows and axes against horses and guns. Today, almost 500 years later, much of our history has been forgotten.

This is where my passion for identity was born. Being the CEO of my company, as an audiovisual advertiser, I had to take advantage of all the culture that surrounded me and transmit it through my work. Peru is a rich country in every sense. there are many things to enjoy.

I grew up in Lima, Peru and all the things I love about my country, the mysticism, the food, beautiful landscapes and the warrior culture, are all a part of who I am.
